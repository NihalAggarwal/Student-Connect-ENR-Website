Identifying the Customers:

Primary Customer: Students of Tier-2 and Tier-3 Colleges.
They are the focus TG.
Secondary Customers:
The Parents of the Students:
They are emotionally attached to their children and they are well aware of the career of Student is at Stake in the University.
The Universities (Tier-2 & Tier-3):
The Universities are a huge Stake Holder in this even if they are not directly Involved with the Product. The reason is if the student culture flourishes and it gives rise to a better cohort of Students passing out year on year then the University is ultimately going to benefit from it by getting quality intake of Students. The Recruiters
The Recruiters can also benefit from getting industry competent employable Students

Pain Areas for the Customers

Identifying Pain Areas assists considerably for eradicating the existing problems faced by the customers

Connectivity among the Community
  • The students are facing difficulty contacting and approaching seniors due to lack of socialability and camaraderie
  • Many times, students who possess expertise in their specific domain are not able to easily share their knowledge with other students
  • The students often are not well connected with seniors on a personal and professional level
Academic Insecurities
  • The students generally take a lot of time to become comfortable and lack of interaction makes the life of student quite difficult
  • The nature of students who exhibit introverted behavior finds it difficult to interact with their seniors in person as well as fellow batchmates
  • Students might face peer pressure and are unable to communicate effectively.
Lack of Proper Guidance
  • Students often feel a lack of guidance and proper academic direction with respect to Top Tier Universities and Colleges
  • The students sometimes have extremely steep learning curves due to lack of proper guidance
  • There exists lack of proper platforms where students could learn and interact
  • The students are spending too much time researching and looking out for internships and job openings
Ineffective Communication
  • Many students have language problems or speech difficulty so they might face issues during the interaction
  • The students are facing difficulty receiving support from their peers in the college community
  • A lot of right sets of circumstances are missed by students just due to lack of effective comunication and they might miss on potential breakthroughs

  • Lack of User Friendly Chat Systems among college community hinders social networking and ease of accessibility among fellow students
  • Lack of broadcating of various college events, club and social activities and workshops hampers student productivity

Identification of the Problem Definition

After a meticulous process, finally we could draft a final problem statement

  • During student’s college life especially in tier 2/3 colleges, there is a lack of effective communication between students of the same batch as well as their seniors and students from other disciplines due to this, students are missing out a lot of internships and job openings, and are unable to seek guidance
