Final Featured Product

Final Product

Our primary goal was to improvise on the Exisitng prototype 2 but and make the product Product feature rich and free of vulnerabilities and bugs. Apart from intensive testing, fixing of bugs worked on the idea of Facilitating Cohort based Learning. We have been able to make some progress in terms of making Groups where everyone in the College Community can ask questions and Post Queries. We have also added some in App Gamification in forms of likes which is an added motivator for people to answer questions.

Our Main Features

Facilitating Cohort Based Learning

Learning from Like Minded and more experienced people in a domain will enrich the learning experience of every student

Post Queries and Questions.Get them answered real Quick

This will be the place to ask questions and queries. Since the Platform is Exclusive to the College Community your questions get answered much faster. Also increases the Scope of asking personalized questions regarding specifis of your College .

The process of Integrating the Groups Feature features and Technology that drives it

We first started with the reviews that we got after the second prototype which was to make groups based on the subjects/domains of interest of Students. Once we were able to make those Groups we made added the feature of Asking Questions in the group related to that Topic. Fellow College Community members who are well versed with the the topic can then help them in getting their questions resolved. We have also added a touch of gamification in terms of likes so that there is added motivation to answer questions to fellow Group members.This is a small way we have incentivised members.
